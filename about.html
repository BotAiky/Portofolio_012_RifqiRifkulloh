ABOUT US

PT. Aiky Company is a professionally managed Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) company. Engaged in the sale of handicrafts. Cooperating with home industry craftsmen. Our main products are handicrafts made from mendong. The handicraft products that we offer are of high quality at competitive and affordable prices.

VISI

To become the best company in the handicraft business by continuously innovating to create products that are environmentally friendly, have artistic and aesthetic values and become a company that cares and benefits the surrounding environment and society.

MISI

  • To become a professional company in presenting products, transaction security and legality.
  • Become a company that has ethics, manners, and is beneficial to the surrounding environment.
  • Adding employment opportunities for people around and improving the quality of life for craftsmen.
